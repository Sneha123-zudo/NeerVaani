Waterfootprint Calculator

Users can calculate their personal water footprint by responding to a series of straightforward questions about their daily activities, such as the types of food they consume, their water usage habits, and lifestyle choices. This interactive process provides a clear understanding of how much water is used directly and indirectly in their routine, helping them identify areas where they can reduce consumption and adopt more sustainable practices.
